# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free community.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- being respectful in disagreement
- accepting feedback in good faith
- focusing discussion on the work
- helping others learn without gatekeeping

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or conduct
- trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact details listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
